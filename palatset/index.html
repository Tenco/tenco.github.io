Offering a creative work experience

Palatset offers tailored workshops, meetings, events, conferences, and team building experiences for up to 40 people.

The space is completely modular, with a stage, conference room, kitchen/ buffet area, outdoor terrace, cozy library lounge, piano cocktail area, presentation and projection areas, and plenty of wall space to draw, design and collaborate on.

We also offer experienced designers, innovation managers and facilitators who can support you in planning and running engaging meetings and workshops, in line with your organisation’s goals. Catering and after work packages are also available.

Brain

We can support organisations by planning and facilitating workshops and meetings, brainstorming and ideation sessions, and team building activities at Palatset.

Space

Palatset can be rented for a full or half day, with full access to all areas in the 140m2 space.

Materials Included

Wifi, projector, whiteboards and blackboards, fika, Sonos™ sound system, device charging station, LEGO™, and writing and drawing materials are included in every booking.
